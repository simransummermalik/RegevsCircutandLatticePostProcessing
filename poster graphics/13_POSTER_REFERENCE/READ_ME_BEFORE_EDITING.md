# Reference poster audit

`original_poster_page1_REFERENCE_ONLY.png` is a reduced preview of page 1 of
the supplied 48×36-inch poster PDF. It is a reference, not a finished graphic.

Before presenting or exporting the poster:

- export **page 1 only**; pages 2–3 of the PDF are untouched Illinois template
  instructions and a logo sheet;
- delete all ANN/BNN, biological-neuron, and evolutionary-optimization text;
- replace the empty Results panel with the frozen certificate-gap results;
- remove or replace the missing `N=100,091` circuit caption;
- finish the incomplete Regev and QFT explanations;
- do not say “dequantizing the QFT” unless the term is precisely defined—the
  repository performs expensive classical finite simulation, not a general
  complexity-theoretic dequantization;
- qualify any Regev asymptotic comparison by resource and assumptions;
- use the companion implementation as prior engineering work, not as held-out
  evidence for the current QFT result.

The graphics library keeps the poster's navy/orange institutional lineage but
adds blue, cyan, purple, and teal encodings for exact QFT, the two finite state
models, and empirical non-inferiority.
